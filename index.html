Internal Book of Work
AI initiatives in flight across the Operations domain, each following the seven-phase delivery methodology. At Citi, Phases 1–5 are complete on the anchor use case. Phases 6–7 require the agent interface to reach production readiness before activation.
Seven-Phase AI Delivery Methodology · Applied to All Initiatives
01
Discover
Map the AS-IS process step by step, quantify the volume and pain, and document data assets. No technology decision is made at this stage.
02
Define
Translate the pain into a falsifiable value hypothesis with specific KPIs. Lock the risk tier and human oversight designation. Business Director sign-off required before engineering is engaged.
03
Build
Build the minimum viable agent scoped to the highest-value part of the workflow. Test against an SME ground truth dataset. Scope discipline is the primary constraint.
04
Contain
Deploy to a cohort of 5-15 people running in parallel with the existing process. Real data, contained blast radius. Fix only, no new features during this phase.
05
Evaluate
Present the Go, Scale or Stop recommendation with the full evidence pack. The recommendation belongs to the programme lead. The decision belongs to the Business Director.
06
Scale
Phased rollout across teams or geographies with evidence gates between each phase. KPI confirmation required before the next phase opens. Pass the gate, then grow.
07
Operate
Continuous monitoring, automated drift detection and retraining triggers. Quarterly performance review to the COO. An agent is never done.
